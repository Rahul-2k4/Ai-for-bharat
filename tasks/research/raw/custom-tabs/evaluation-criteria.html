Evaluation Criteria

Jury Composition

Submissions will be evaluated by a diverse and highly experienced jury panel comprising:

  • Domain Mentors (1 per track) — Experts with deep sector knowledge

  • Technical Mentors (2 per track) — AI/ML specialists assessing technical depth

  • Government Officials (IAS officers & pilot sponsors) — Evaluating real-world feasibility and policy alignment

  • Investor Jury — Representatives from leading venture capital firms responsible for evaluating scalability and market potential.

Evaluation Criteria

Each submission will be assessed across the following dimensions:

  • Problem Relevance & Depth of Understanding — 20%

  • Technical Implementation & Innovation — 25%

  • Real-World Deployability & Government Feasibility — 25%

  • Demo Quality & Presentation — 15%

  • Scalability & Long-Term Impact — 15%

Screening Process

For the online phase, submissions will be evaluated using HackerEarth’s standard assessment framework to shortlist the Top 50 teams advancing to the Grand Finale.
