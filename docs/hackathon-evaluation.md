# AI for Bharat 2 - Evaluation Criteria (Offline Copy)

Last verified: 2026-04-15 (Asia/Kolkata)

## Jury Composition

- Domain Mentors (1 per track): deep sector knowledge.
- Technical Mentors (2 per track): AI/ML technical depth assessment.
- Government Officials (IAS officers and pilot sponsors): feasibility and policy alignment.
- Investor Jury: scalability and market potential.

## Scoring Dimensions

- Problem Relevance and Depth of Understanding: 20%
- Technical Implementation and Innovation: 25%
- Real-World Deployability and Government Feasibility: 25%
- Demo Quality and Presentation: 15%
- Scalability and Long-Term Impact: 15%

## Screening Process (Online Phase)

- HackerEarth standard assessment framework is used for online screening.
- Top 50 teams are shortlisted to advance to the Grand Finale.
